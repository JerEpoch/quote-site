What is this?

This will be a collection of some of the most ridiculous quotes from Trump's speeches.

Ok, but why?

Because his speeches are almost pure nonsense. They might sound semi good when he speaks them. But looking at the text is pure gibberish in parts. I thought it would be funny to highlight some.

But why 5 year olds?

This originally started as a fun quiz site of determining if Trump or a 5 year old said it. However, I feel like 5 year olds are fairly smart. It would have been too easy to guess as a result. Now it's just Trump quotes.

Well, all quotes are fully sourced. So you can see for yourself. If you still feel so, you should examine to why you feel there's a bias.
